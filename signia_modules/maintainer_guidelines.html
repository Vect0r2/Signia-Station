Maintainers

  • These guidelines apply to maintainers of the Signia codebase. Maintainers have commit access to the repository, that means they can merge pull requests opened by contributors. Maintainers guide the balance of the game and review code of contributors to make sure it's up to standard. Just please act in good faith
